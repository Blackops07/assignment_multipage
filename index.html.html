Arthur Shelby

Arthur Shelby is Thomas Shelby's older brother and a key member of the Peaky Blinders gang. Known for his fiery temper and loyalty to the family, Arthur often acts as Thomas's right-hand man. Despite his struggles with inner demons, his bond with Thomas remains strong and unwavering.

Polly Gray

Polly Gray, the matriarch of the Shelby family, serves as the treasurer of the Peaky Blinders. Her wisdom and keen business acumen make her an invaluable advisor to Thomas. Polly's fierce protectiveness and love for her family often guide her decisions, making her a formidable presence in their lives.

Grace Burgess

Grace Burgess initially enters Thomas Shelby's life as an undercover agent but soon becomes his love interest. Their complex relationship is marked by passion, betrayal, and deep emotional connection. Grace's influence on Thomas is profound, impacting both his personal and professional life.
